## Research Findings

### Successful App Models (Proven in US/Europe/Asia):

1. **TaskRabbit/Thumbtack** - Local services marketplace
   - Revenue: $75M (TaskRabbit) - $231M (Thumbtack) annually
   - Connects customers with local service providers
   - 2008 launch, now billion-dollar valuations

2. **HopSkipDrive** - Safe school transportation
   - Started by 3 mothers in Los Angeles
   - Partners with school districts for student transport
   - Real-time tracking, vetted "CareDrivers"

3. **Waze Carpool** - Community-based ride sharing
   - Successful trials in California, Washington, Israel
   - Matches drivers/riders on similar routes
   - Nominal fee for gas cost sharing

4. **BusyKid/Greenlight** - Kids chore & allowance apps
   - Millions of users in USA
   - Chore tracking + financial education
   - Kids earn, save, donate, invest
   - Strong proven market demand

5. **Carpool Arabia** - General ride-sharing in UAE
   - 21% month-on-month growth since 2013
   - BUT focuses on adult commuting, NOT school transport
